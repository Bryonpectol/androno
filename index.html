Andromeda Cards

Andromeda is a card game where the players collect physical cards to build their deck. Each player starts the game with one commander. The commander card has a barcode placed on the card. The player must scan the barcode and will be directed to that commander's webpage. The commander's webpage will tell the player all the stats, abilities, and other content of the commander.



The Spaceship card is another key card in the game. Your spaceship is what you are trying to defend each game. Your spaceship has a life total. You win the game by getting your opponent's spaceship's life total down to zero.



Planet Cards are the battlefield cards. Every game you play, you and your opponent are fighting over the planet. At the end of the game, whoever wins, now owns that planet.



Fleet Ship Cards are smaller spaceships that you use to battle and attack you're opponent's spaceship.
